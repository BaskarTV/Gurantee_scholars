BA DEFENCE

About Course

BA Defence Studies is a Under Graduate Defence Studies course. The course is three years program that includes a comprehensive and thorough study of Defence Strategic Studies as a discipline at an advanced level, & This course certification is approved by UGC (University Grant Commission of India)

What is the eligibility for BA Defence?

The basic eligibility criteria for pursuing B.A. Defence Studies course is a pass degree in Higher Secondary Examination or 10+2 from a recognized college or university. They should have obtained a minimum of 50% marks in the qualifying examination.

What is the scope of BA Defence?

After clearing the UPSC examination after your graduation, one can be recruited as a 'Commission Officer' in the Indian Army, Indian Air Force or Indian Navy. After completing Ph. D./Research Fellowship in Defence/Military Studies, one can become a Professor/Lecturer in renowned universities/Institutes in India.

Is Defence a good career?

Of all professions, a career in defence is the most respected, noblest and indeed the most sacred one. Having a career in defence makes a person not only disciplined and strong but also opens up many other avenues for growth

What are the subjects in BA Defence?

Geopolitics and Military Geography, Science and Technology, Defense Economics, Conflict Management and Conflict Resolution, International Law, International Relations, Science and Technology, Area Studies, Nuclear Policies, and other topics are covered in Defence & Strategic Studies.
