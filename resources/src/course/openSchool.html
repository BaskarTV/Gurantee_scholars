Board of Open School Secondry & Skills Education

BOSSE is open schooling education in Sikkim, Established underact no.12 of 2020 by the Sikkim Legislative Assembly,Passed on 21-02-2020 according to the Sikkim Act 2020 to promulgate and disseminate the open schooling education at state & national level

The Board of Open Schooling and Skill Education (BOSSE),Skkim is an open schooling board that aims to cater to the varied academic needs of the divergent group of students up to pre-degree level including Secondary/Senio Secondary, skill and vocational education

The Uttar Pradesh State Open School Board

The Uttar Pradesh State Open School Board (UPSOSB) is an autonomous board running under the auspices of Open Schooling of Uttar Pradesh Goverment’s Education Department Following the rules and regulations of The Uttar Pradesh State Open School Board Act.2008 U.P Act No.27 of 2008 As Passed by the Uttar Pradesh Legislature Dated-29 August 2008

UPSOSB provides educational opportunties to persons like you who wist to study further and qualify for a better tomorrow The mission of UPSOSB is to provide education to all with special concern to school girls and women rural youth working men and women who because of one or the other reason could not continue their education with the formal system
